How To Book Bus Tickets


  1. Enter the departure and destination locations in the “From” and To tab respectively.
  2. Select the “Date of Your Bus Trip” and hit the “Search” button.
  3. Select a bus service of your choice from the list of buses that are displayed then click bookme
  4. Select a seat number and mention your gender in the seat plan.
  5. Put in the “Passenger Information” and “Contact Information”
  6. You have to mention whether you are vaccinated or not
  7. You will receive the OTP on your phone number, put it in.
  8. After filling all the information, select the payment method i.e easy paisa, debit card, and jazz cash etc.
